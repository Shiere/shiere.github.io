Pokemon
  Pokemon is a media franchise managed by The Pokemon Company, a Japanese consortium between Nintendo, Game Freak. The franchise was created by Satoshi Tajiri in 1995, and is centered on fictional creatures call "Pokemon", which humans, known as Pokemon Trainers, catch and train to battle each other for sport.
